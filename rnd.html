Project- Smart Lens Eye-Tracking

Status: In Development

Current VR systems rely on headset-mounted cameras for eye-tracking, which can be imprecise and computationally heavy. We are developing 'Project Drishti', a system that integrates micro-sensors directly into smart contact lenses. This allows for unparalleled tracking accuracy of eye-gaze and pupil dilation, leading to truly realistic depth-of-field, foveated rendering, and a more intuitive user interface for the metaverse.

Potential Impact: Reduced motion sickness, lower processing power requirements, and a hyper-realistic immersive experience.
